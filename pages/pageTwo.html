Sebastian's Favorite Lego Top Ten

10. Mars Mission MB-01 Eagle Command Base

This set is one I remember quite fondly as a child (never owned it, just saw it), but looking back on it, its not the greatest. Not much is offered outside the gimmicky air pump, but it is still a nice theme and good set.

9. Dino Triceratops Trapper

A simple set with a simple premise, but a solid and functional build makes this great to mess around with. Do I have nostagia bias? Noo.

8. Ultra Agents Ocean HQ

I love big boats and I cannot lie. I was never crazy over the Ultra Agents theme, but they certainly produced some of the most advanced, fun-packed sets Lego has ever made. This is certainly one of them.

7. Star Wars Executor Super Star Destroyer

Once again, never been a huge fan of Star Wars. That just makes this little beauty all the more impressive to catch my eye. Well designed, well-executed, and something I'd actually be willing to buy.

6. Mega Bloks Destiny Fallen Walker

I'm cheating including a set that isnt from the actual Lego brand, but this is a set my brother used to own. The coolest thing with tons of functionality. That paired with me being an OG Destiny fan makes this set one of my favorites.

5. Ninjago Ultra Stealth Raider

A bulky set that is only one of two on this list that I actually still have fully built and on my desk. Its a huge tank-like thing (not very stealthy if you ask me) that seperates into 4 smaller vehicles. An incredible build, very fun to use to this day.

4. Chima Lion Chi Temple

Now this is a big boy. A flagship set, hosting everything you would want in one package. I actually owned this, though its been so long its a hazy memory. Just fantastic really, wish I still had it.

3. Chima Worriz's Combat Lair

Another set from the Legends of Chima theme that takes a spot on this list. Honestly thinking about it, the Chima theme really made some great sets, which from an outside perspective I would never expect. Still wish I had this one too.

2. Cobi USS Pensylvania-Class Battleship

Another set I'm cheating with, as it isn't from Lego. This is the second of these sets that I still have, mainly because its the most recent one I bought. Big boat, battleship, many pieces, very cool.

1. Power Miners Titanium Command Rig

The magnum opus, the Power Miners Titanium Command Rig. An older set from Lego, but probably one of their finest to ever come out, featuring plenty of functionality to keep you entertained for as long as you want. For as long as I remember I've always wanted one of these sets, and even now looking at it, it aged pretty well. A bit flimsey, but you literally lift the drill bit and the center of the vehicle so that it 'drills' vertically. That's amazing. Wow.
